Vote 'Yes' on January 10th to Continue Ada's Progress.

Proposition 2 is a quarter of a cent sales tax initiative which funds economic development in Ada, Oklahoma. The election to renew Proposition 2 is will extend our current quarter cent sales tax. It is neither a new tax nor a tax increase. This initiative has been successfully extended by voters 3 times, and the vote to extend the initiative for a 4th time will come this January 10th. A vote 'YES' will help continue our success, invest in the community, and prepare our economy for the future.

Proposition 2 helps fund economic development efforts throughout Ada. Ada has a local organization to support and organize economic development called the Ada Jobs Foundation. The Jobs Foundation supports efforts in business retention and expansion, business attraction, entrepreneurship, and community development. Proposition 2 also funds retail attraction efforts. The Ada Jobs Foundation is recognized as the smallest Accredited Economic Development Organization by the International Economic Development Council, an achievement given to only 53 organizations in North America.
