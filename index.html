Proposition 2 is sales tax initiative which funds economic development in Ada, Oklahoma. It was started in 1998 has been sucessfully renewed by voters 3 times since then. The vote to renew the initiative for a 4th time will come this January. We are asking Ada citizens to 'YES' to continue our sucess, invest in our community, and help our economy grow.
